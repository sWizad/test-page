Validation Loss

In conclusion, deaper level of coarse-to-fine is better amd slower decay is better. coarse-to-fine can also reduces computational time.

Experiment 5: mpi interpolation


Validation Loss
We compare the result between different number of sharing mpi and degree of approximated mpi.
We train every model with 40 mpi and share mpic 1 mpic per N mpia. For N = 2, 4, 8. And degree of approximated mpi = 0, 1, 2
In conlusion, higher degree longer time to compute. There are no significant improvement when incresas the degree until the number of shared layer is high. Higher number of shared layer fewer time to compute but a bit reduces the quality.
